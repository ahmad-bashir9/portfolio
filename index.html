About

I am a Machine Learning Engineer with over 3 years of experience specializing in computer vision, deep learning, and real-time video processing. With a BS in Computer Science from Information Technology University (ITU) Lahore, my passion lies in leveraging AI to build and optimize cutting-edge solutions.

Currently, as a Machine Learning Engineer at Colossyan, I lead innovative projects focusing on generative AI and 3D vision. My core technical expertise includes developing and optimizing video generation models using PyTorch and diffusion techniques, implementing deep learning for AI avatars, gesture synthesis, and speech synchronization, and improving the efficiency of real-time video processing.

Prior to this, I spent two years as a Research Assistant in the Intelligent Machines Lab where I pioneered PyTorch-based models for 3D reconstruction and NeRF preprocessing, even contributing to a CVPR paper submission. I'm constantly exploring the latest developments in GenAI.
